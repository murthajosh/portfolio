UX Design

I take a user-centered approach to web and product design, because understanding users means understanding business, and helping users succeed is how businesses succeed.

UI Design

An interface should work like users expect it to work without adding more complication to their lives. The best digital interfaces fade into the background, making way for people to get things done.

Front-End Development

The user-centered mindset doesn't end at handoff. I try my best to write front-end code that's accessible to all users, and maintainable by colleagues and future developers.
